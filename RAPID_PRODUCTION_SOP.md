# 🚀 RAPID PRODUCTION SOP
*Zero-Decision Video Publishing System*
**Version 1.0 | Created: Feb 20, 2026**

---

## ⚡ THE 15-MINUTE VIDEO PIPELINE

### BEFORE YOU START
- [ ] Open CapCut mobile app
- [ ] Have gameplay video ready (downloads folder)
- [ ] Have voiceover MP3 ready (ElevenLabs or GTTS)
- [ ] Know your hook text (from script)

---

## STEP 1: IMPORT (2 min)
1. Tap **"New Project"**
2. Select your **gameplay video**
3. Tap **"Add"**
4. Tap **"Audio" → "Extract"** (if audio is separate)
5. OR tap **"Audio" → "Sounds" → import voiceover MP3
6. Sync voiceover to video start (trim if needed)

---

## STEP 2: ADD HOOK TEXT (3 min)

**Hook Requirements:**
- First 3 seconds ONLY
- Maximum 8 words
- Pattern: "I [shocking action]" or "This [unexpected thing]"
- Color: ORANGE (#FF6B35)
- Font: Bold, 64pt size
- Position: Center screen

**Example Hooks:**
- "I scammed my scammer back"
- "This app saved me $10K"
- "I caught my partner cheating"

**How to add:**
1. Tap **"Text" → "Add Text"**
2. Type your hook
3. Tap **Style** → Select bold font
4. **Color:** Orange
5. **Size:** 64pt
6. **Stroke:** Black, 4pt thickness
7. **Position:** Center
8. **Duration:** Exactly 3 seconds (adjust text clip length)

---

## STEP 3: ADD BODY CAPTIONS (5 min)

**Caption Requirements:**
- White text (#FFFFFF)
- Font: Bold, 54pt size
- Black stroke, 3pt thickness
- Position: Bottom third of screen
- Sync with voiceover timing
- Break into short phrases (3-5 words max per caption)

**How to add:**
1. Tap **"Text" → "Add Text"**
2. Type first phrase
3. **Style:** Bold, White, 54pt
4. **Stroke:** Black, 3pt
5. **Position:** Bottom third
6. Adjust timing to match voiceover
7. **Duplicate** text layer for next phrase (saves time)
8. Edit text, adjust timing
9. Repeat for all phrases

**Pro Tip:** Use Auto-Captions as starting point, then edit for accuracy

---

## STEP 4: ADD CTA (1 min)

**CTA Requirements:**
- Last 3-5 seconds
- Text: "Part 2 at [X] followers!" or "Link in bio for full story"
- Color: Yellow or Orange
- Size: 48pt

**How to add:**
1. Add text at end of video
2. Style: Bold, Yellow/Orange
3. Duration: 3-5 seconds

---

## STEP 5: EXPORT (2 min)

**CRITICAL SETTINGS:**
1. Tap **"Export"** (top right)
2. **Resolution:** 1080p (select if not default)
3. **Frame Rate:** 30fps (NOT 60fps)
4. **Bitrate:** HIGH (this is the key)
5. **Format:** MP4

**Why 30fps?**
- TikTok often converts 60fps to 30fps anyway
- 30fps = more bitrate per frame = sharper text
- Smaller file = faster upload

---

## STEP 6: UPLOAD TO TIKTOK (2 min)

**Upload Checklist:**
1. Open TikTok app
2. Tap **+** → **Upload** (not record)
3. Select exported video from gallery
4. **BEFORE posting:**
   - [ ] Add caption with keywords
   - [ ] Add 3-5 relevant hashtags
   - [ ] Enable "High Quality Uploads" in settings
   - [ ] Tag video with relevant sounds/trends
5. Tap **Post**

**Caption Formula:**
```
[Hook restated as question]

Part 2 when this hits [X] likes!

#redditstories #storytime #viral #fyp #[niche-specific]
```

---

## 📊 QUALITY CHECKLIST

Before hitting export, verify:
- [ ] Hook text is ORANGE and VISIBLE in first 3s
- [ ] Captions are WHITE with BLACK stroke
- [ ] Text doesn't go off-screen
- [ ] Voiceover is clear and synced
- [ ] Gameplay is smooth (no lag)
- [ ] CTA is at the end
- [ ] Total length: 30-60 seconds

---

## 🎯 BATCH WORKFLOW (For Multiple Videos)

**Efficient Approach:**
1. Do Steps 1-6 for Video 001
2. Export and upload
3. Immediately start Video 002 (CapCut stays open)
4. Same process, different content
5. Aim for 3-4 videos per session

**Why this works:**
- Muscle memory builds after first video
- No re-learning the interface
- Momentum carries through

---

## 🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING

**Problem: Text looks blurry after upload**
→ Export at HIGH bitrate, ensure 30fps, upload from gallery (not forwarded)

**Problem: Voiceover out of sync**
→ Re-import audio, check video framerate matches audio

**Problem: Video too long**
→ Trim gameplay to match voiceover length (30-60s total)

**Problem: Export fails**
→ Close other apps, free up storage, try lower resolution then upscale
