## 🔗 CREATE YOUR FIRST AFFILIATE LINK

### Step 1: Log into Associates Dashboard
**URL:** https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/home

### Step 2: Find Products to Promote

**For Reddit Story Niche, promote:**
- **Books:** "Crazy Rich Asians", "The 48 Laws of Power", psychology books
- **Audible:** Free trial (easy conversion)
- **Kindle:** E-readers for bookworms
- **Wireless Earbuds:** For content creators
- **Ring Lights:** For TikTok creators
- **Phone Tripods:** For mobile creators

### Step 3: Generate Link

**Method 1: SiteStripe**
1. Visit any Amazon product page
2. Click "Get Link" toolbar (SiteStripe)
3. Copy text link or image link
4. Shorten in Linktree

**Method 2: Product Links Tool**
1. Go to Associates Dashboard
2. Click "Product Links"
3. Search product name or ASIN
4. Click "Get Link"
5. Copy URL

### Step 4: Shorten with Linktree
1. Go to linktr.ee
2. Create free account
3. Add Amazon affiliate links
4. Add title (e.g., "Books I Recommend")
5. Save

## ⚠️ RULES TO FOLLOW

### DO:
- ✅ Disclose affiliate links ("Link in bio contains affiliate links")
- ✅ Only recommend products you know/trust
- ✅ Make content genuinely helpful
- ✅ Track what converts

### DON'T:
- ❌ Spam links everywhere
- ❌ Recommend products you've never seen
- ❌ Promise specific results
- ❌ Use link shorteners that hide it's Amazon

### FTC Disclosure Requirement
**Must include in bio or caption:**
```
"Links in bio contain affiliate links. I may earn commission from purchases."
```

**Or shorter:**
```
"#affiliate #ad"
```
