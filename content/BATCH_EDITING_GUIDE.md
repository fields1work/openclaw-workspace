## ðŸ­ The Assembly Line Method

### Phase 1: Import & Sync (15 min total)

**Do this for ALL 5 videos before editing:**

1. **Create 5 new projects** in CapCut (name them clearly)
2. **Import all 10 voiceovers** to each project? Noâ€”just import relevant voiceover
3. Actually: Import voiceover + 5-8 stock clips per project
4. **Drag voiceover to timeline first** (this sets your video length)
5. **Sync markers:** Add markers on timeline at hook points (see storyboards)

### Phase 2: Edit Video 1-5 Assembly Line (90 min)

**Work in this order PER VIDEO:**

1. **Rough Cut** (3 min): Place footage in order per storyboard
2. **Sync to Audio** (5 min): Match cuts to voiceover beats/pauses
3. **Add Text/Captions** (5 min): Hook text + captions per storyboard
4. **Effects/Transitions** (3 min): Color grade + transitions
5. **Audio Mix** (2 min): Voiceover 100%, music 20-30%
6. **Export** (set running, move to next video while it processes)

**Don't perfect one videoâ€”get all 5 to "good enough" first, then polish if time remains.**
