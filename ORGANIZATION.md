## Workflow

### Daily Loop:
1. **Junior Analyst** monitors all channels 24/7
2. **CTO (Aneko)** analyzes hourly data in Telegram
3. **CEO (Fields)** reviews executive summary
4. **CEO** gives strategic direction
5. **CTO** implements via bot or automation

### Example:
- Someone posts in #❓｜help-desk: "How do I get viral?"
- Junior Analyst logs it, flags for CTO
- CTO alerts CEO via Telegram: "Strategic question needs your input"
- CEO responds: "Tell them to post 100 videos before asking"
- CTO commands bot to post CEO's wisdom
