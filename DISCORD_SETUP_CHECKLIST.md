2. Click "+" on left sidebar
3. "Create My Own" → "For me and my friends"
4. **Name:** `AI Income HQ`
5. Upload icon (robot + $ or AI-themed)
6. Set description: `Building passive income with AI — $0 → $10K/month`

## STEP 4: SET PERMISSIONS (10 min)

### Make #welcome, #rules, #roadmap Read-Only:
1. Right-click channel → "Edit Channel" → "Permissions"
2. @everyone → Send Messages: ❌
3. Save

### Make #announcements Admin-Only:
1. Same as above
2. Add your role → Send Messages: ✅
