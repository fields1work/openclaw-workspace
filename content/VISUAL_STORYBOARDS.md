## ðŸ”§ CapCut Quick Tips

### General Settings for All Videos
- **Resolution:** 1080 x 1920 (9:16 vertical)
- **Frame Rate:** 60fps
- **Export Quality:** High/Highest
- **Format:** MP4

### Where to Get Stock Footage
1. **Pexels** (free) - pexels.com
2. **Pixabay** (free) - pixabay.com
3. **CapCut Stock** (built-in) - Free with CapCut
4. **TikTok** (rip with permission trends)

### Text Animation Favorites
- **Typewriter** - For dialog/messages
- **Pop** - For hooks/emphasis
- **Counting** - For statistics
- **Glitch** - For disturbing/horror content

### Audio Mix
- Voiceover: 100% (lead)
- Background music: 20-30% (support)
- Sound effects: 40-50% (punch/transition)

### Caption Template
- Font: Montserrat Bold or Arial Bold
- Size: Large (mobile readable)
- Stroke: 4-6px black outline
- Safe zone: Keep text in middle 60% of screen (TikTok UI covers edges)
