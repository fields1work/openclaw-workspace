### The Hook (First 3 seconds - CRITICAL)
- Flash text: "Storytime" or "Wait for it..."
- Or start with the most shocking line visible immediately
- Use bold, readable font (Montserrat Bold or similar)

### Body (Middle section)
- Cut between:
  - Static text cards with key story beats
  - Stock footage that matches the mood
  - Occasional emojis/animations for emphasis
- Keep visuals changing every 3-5 seconds to maintain attention

### Ending (Last 5 seconds)
- Text: "Like + follow for part 2" or "Drop a üíÄ if you would have done the same"

## üé® Visual Style by Niche

### Reddit Stories
- Background: Subtle gradient or blurred subreddit screenshot
- Text: White with black outline, centered
- Footage: Generic relationship/couple stock footage
- Music: Emotional/suspenseful trending audio (find on TikTok "trending sounds")

### Disturbing Facts
- Background: Dark aesthetic, bedroom/house footage
- Text: White or red, glitch effect optional
- Footage: Claustrophobic/sleep-related imagery
- Music: Eerie ambient or trending creepy audio

### Workplace Drama
- Background: Office setting, desk, laptop
- Text: White with subtle shadow
- Footage: Corporate stock footage, typing, meetings
- Music: Corporate drama or trending audio
