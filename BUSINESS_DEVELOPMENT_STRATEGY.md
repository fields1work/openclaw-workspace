### TIKTOK CREATOR REWARDS PROGRAM

**Current Requirements (2025-2026):**
- 10,000+ followers
- 100,000+ views in past 30 days
- Post original content (1+ minute videos)
- Account in good standing

**Payout Structure:**
- $0.40 - $1.00 per 1,000 views (RPM)
- Pays monthly via PayPal or Stripe
- Rewards videos 1+ minute in length (perfect for 60s stories)

**Strategy:**
- Post 1 video/day minimum (algorithm boost)
- Target 50-100K monthly views = $50-100/month
- Scale to 500K views = $200-500/month
- At 1M views = $400-1000/month (baseline)

**Reddit Stories Advantage:**
- Longer videos = higher RPM
- Niche content = loyal audience
- Repost potential = viral accumulation

## Tactic 2: The "Part 2" Viral Loop

**Strategy:**
- Every story ends with cliffhanger
- "Part 2 drops at 10K followers"
- Creates engagement pressure
- Followers share to see resolution

**Engagement Multiplier:**
- Comments: "DROP PART 2" drives algorithm
- Shares: "You won't believe this"
- Likes: Social proof
- Follows: Anticipation of closure

---

## Tactic 3: Cross-Platform Synergy

**The Funnel:**
1. **TikTok:** Awareness (viral content)
2. **Instagram:** Engagement (daily stories)
3. **Twitter:** Authority (long-form thoughts)
4. **YouTube:** Monetization (longer content)
5. **Email List:** Ownership (direct access)

**Content Recycling:**
- One story = TikTok + Reels + Shorts + Tweet thread
- 4x the content from 1x the effort
- Each platform reinforces the others

---

## Tactic 4: Automation Before Scale

**Priority:**
1. Build manual process first (understand what works)
2. Document every step
3. Automate repetitive parts
4. Outsource low-value tasks
5. Scale what works

**Tools to Consider:**
- **Notion:** Content calendar
- **Zapier/Make:** Workflow automation
- **CapCut:** Video editing
- **ElevenLabs:** Voiceover
- **Canva:** Thumbnails
- **Buffer:** Cross-platform posting
- **ChatGPT:** Script assistance

# ⚠️ RISK MITIGATION

## Common Failure Points

### Algorithm Dependency Risk
- **Problem:** TikTok policy change = income loss
- **Solution:** Build email list, diversify platforms

### Burnout Risk
- **Problem:** Constant creation = exhaustion
- **Solution:** Batch content, schedule posts, take breaks

### Quality vs Quantity
- **Problem:** Rushing = poor content
- **Solution:** Templates + systems = 15 min/video

### Monetization Delay
- **Problem:** No revenue = motivation drop
- **Solution:** Affiliate first (immediate $), scale later

### Competition
- **Problem:** Others doing same thing
- **Solution:** Personal brand + systems = moat
