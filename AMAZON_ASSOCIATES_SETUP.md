### Step 1: Create Account (3 min)

4. Enter basic info:
   - Name: Your name
   - Address: Your address

- Topics: Entertainment, Books, Technology
- Traffic: Social Media

- Primary: 18-34 years old

- How you drive traffic: TikTok videos, link in bio

**Monetization:**
- Purpose: Recommend products I use
- Current methods: Affiliate links
- Expected monthly traffic: 1,000-10,000 clicks

### Step 3: Identity Verification (5 min)
- Enter tax information (SSN for US)
- Choose payment method:
  - Direct deposit (fastest)
  - Amazon gift card
  - Check by mail
- Verify identity (may need to submit ID)

### Step 4: Immediate Access (2 min)
- Get Associates ID: something like `fieldsco-20`

## CREATING YOUR FIRST AFFILIATE LINK

### Method 1: SiteStripe (Easiest)
1. Browse Amazon normally
2. See "Amazon Associates SiteStripe" at top
3. Click "Text" or "Image" or "Text+Image"
4. Copy generated link
5. Shorten with amzn.to

### Method 2: Link Generator
1. Go to: `affiliate-program.amazon.com/home/tools/linkchecker`
2. Paste ANY Amazon URL
3. Get affiliate link instantly

### Method 3: Short Links (Best for Social)
1. Use: `amzn.to` links
2. Format: `amzn.to/XXXXXXX`
3. Clean and trackable

## LEGAL REQUIREMENTS

### FTC Disclosure Rules:
- Must disclose affiliate links
- Clear and conspicuous
- Before the link

**How to Disclose:**
1. In video: "Link in bio (affiliate)"
2. In bio: "Some links may be affiliate"
3. In posts: "#ad" or "#affiliate"

**TikTok Safe:**
- Bio disclosure covers all
- Video: "Check link in bio"
- No need for explicit #ad if bio has disclosure
